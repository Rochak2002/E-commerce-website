Effect of Technology in Student's Life

By Rochak Lama | 6th April,2022

Technology has become an everyday aspect of our life during the last two to three decades. We are all surrounded by technology and have become accustomed to it. The majority of our job is done with technology, and we are fully reliant on it. Our lives have been taken over by technology at this point. We are surrounded by them as we look around. Computers, cellphones, televisions, and other electronic devices that we use on a regular basis are all around us. The way we work, communicate, and engage with one another has altered as a result of technological advancements. Furthermore, the rapid spread of smartphones is directly linked to the growing rise of e-commerce and internet-based businesses.
Technology, like anything else, has both positive and harmful aspects. It is not determined by technology. It is up to the user to decide how to utilize it, whether to use it positively or negatively. If technology is not properly used, it may be extremely detrimental to society and the environment. While the smartphone has simplified and revolutionized our job, it has also made us lethargic. Today, you can accomplish a lot of things without leaving your house; everything is at your fingertips and can be done from the comfort of your own home. As a result, we no longer need to go to palaces to work lying, as we did previously.We are so reliant on technology that we end up injuring ourselves by not being fully active in our bodies and not engaging in any physical exercise. This is especially true for the younger generation, since we have become so reliant on technology and spend so much time utilizing it. They are so engrossed in their smartphones that they do not engage in any physical exercise. Smartphone addiction is on the rise in today's world, and it's wreaking havoc on our lives.
Not only are there bad consequences, but there are several favorable consequences as well. Every day, we can see how technology is altering our lives. We have been able to do so much in several disciplines thanks to the use of technology and its assistance. Technology has aided us in better communication; via the use of various technologies, we may speak with individuals who are far away from their homes, as well as communicate from one location to another. The usage of a smartphone allows us to contact anybody in a matter of minutes or seconds, which is quite beneficial to each of us.Similarly, technology has aided us much in the realm of transportation, and there have been several improvements in this area. We can get to other palaces as quickly as possible thanks to technology. Different technical developments, such as aircraft, trains, and metros, have made it easier for us to get to the palace every day. It has altered the way people travel and made things a lot simpler. If we utilize technology correctly, we may greatly benefit the environment as well as the many communities and individuals that live there. Education has undergone a radical transformation as a result of technological advancements. Teaching and learning have been simplified, made more exciting, and made easier to learn.
Technology can help students become more engaged. Education technology can help students better engage with course material by making learning more interactive and collaborative. They learn by doing rather than remembering knowledge. This may be as basic as taking an interactive quiz in class or engaging in tech-enabled group conversations, or it could be as complex as playing educational games, performing science experiments in a virtual lab, or going on a virtual field trip. However, in order to be genuinely interesting, it must be participatory. Using a computer to do arithmetic is no different than using a pencil and pad of paper. Using augmented reality to illustrate arithmetic problems, on the other hand, is a very new ballgame. Interactivity improves the learning experience for certain learners.
Technology may also be used to broaden classroom boundaries; with technology, there are no limits to what can be done in the classroom. Anyone from wherever in the globe may learn and get the same amount of information.
Students who struggled to grasp new topics in a regular classroom would soon lag behind their peers. Students, on the other hand, may work at their own speed with online assignments. Those who want more time or assistance outside of class can do so through guided activities or supplemental coursework. Students may access materials online anytime they need them, and teachers can detect which students might need extra support, thanks to technology's always-on nature. Self-paced learning also aids students in developing digital literacy and 21st-century abilities, which will come in handy once they enter the profession.
There are both positive and bad elements to this, as with all things; it simply depends on who is using it and what he or she is using it for. It has the potential to damage us if not utilized properly, but when used correctly, it has the potential to benefit us in a variety of ways. While technology may be viewed as a danger, it does have its limitations, using it into your teaching practice allows students to interact and connect with course material in new ways. Education is no longer limited to the four walls of your classroom, thanks to technological advancements. YouTube videos and social media don't have to be a source of distraction;  they may actually be used to supplement your course material. It's simple math: greater learning results add up.
